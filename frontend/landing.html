✦ WHY AGENTAQUARIUM

Monitoring That
Actually Makes Sense

You built AI agents. You deserve to watch them in their natural habitat.

🐠
Speed = Activity

Fast-swimming fish are working hard. Slow ones are idle. Spinning ones have errors. Understand your entire fleet in one glance.

⚡
Zero Config

One command. Your tank appears. Agents auto-discovered, auto-arranged. No YAML. No setup wizard. No nonsense.

💬
Click to Chat

Click any fish to DM the agent directly. Interrupt, redirect, or just check in — like tapping the glass.

📊
Deep Metrics

Token usage, API calls, error rates, completion history. Every stat you need, behind one click on any fish.

🔴
Instant Alerts

Error agents glow red and thrash. You'll know something's wrong before you even read a log. Visual-first debugging.

🔒
100% Local

No telemetry. No cloud. No data collection. Your agents' activity stays on your machine, forever.

🚀 HOW IT WORKS

Three Commands to
Your Own Tank

Seriously. Three. That's it.

01
Install

One command and it's done. No dependencies to wrangle, no config to touch.

curl -fsSL aquaticclaw.sh | bash
02
Open Your Tank

Run aquaticclaw and open aquaticclaw — localhost:3333. Your agents are already swimming.

aquaticclaw
03
Watch & Interact

Hover fish for stats. Click to chat. Watch the tank. It's monitoring, but actually pleasant.

aquaticclaw — localhost:3333 🐠
